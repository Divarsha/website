OUR VISION:-

Sapne is working towards developing skills and providing special teaching aids in order to make the knowledge accessible to all. This will be done through our Gurukuls, or Education centres, for children where they can learn, play and get a chance of realizing their sapne(dreams)
The ultimate aim is to create a highly aware generation of youth that is not susceptible to social and financial barriers and can fight their own battles with the power of knowledge and skills

EDUCATION PLATFORM

Our learning platform's which are open to all for learning, playing and other-activities

PLAYGROUND

Our playground is the place where we provide the children to learn in the most innovative ways getting them involved in team Sports/games helps in enhancing their team building skills,social harmonization skills,and shows them the power of unity and group learning. The added advantage of improvement in their motor skills is present as well.

LEARNING

We use various games and visual techniques to teach new topics to the children,thereby making the learning more interesting for them. Through play-way methods they can develop unique social and cognitive skills,mature emotionally and attain the self confidence required to engage in new experiences and enviornment.

SKILL DEVELOPMENT

Knowledge is a necessity,but so is skill development. Skill development course not just enhance a variety of skills in a child but they also increase their self confidence A child without life skills is like a bird who is unaware of how to use its wings. Thus, our skill building courses provide these kids with an optimum platform to launch their dreams!

ACTIVITIES

We believe that true learning comes from activities of body and mind. So,to inculate the spirit of learning and curiosity amongst the children,we engage them in activities like drawing, craft work, dancing,gardening, quizzing. we also organize activities related to general awareness and ethics of life.

CAMPS

**The blood you donate gives someone another chance at life. One day that someone may be a close relative, a friend, a loved one-or even you.**


Through blood donation, we can help various needy people and save their precious life. Blood can be regenerated itself in the body and fulfill its amount, once it is donated but life cannot be got back, once it is gone. A normal and healthy person can easily donate blood many times between 18-60 years of age. Blood donation at the right time can save millions of life all over the world every year. There is very less percentage of healthy people who can donate blood, so please be a voluntary blood donor.




We can pay the best tribute to the father of our nation, Mahatma Gandhi on his 150th birth anniversary by making India a cleaner place to live in." said the Prime Minister Narendra Modi as he launched the Swachh Bharat Abhiyan at Rajpath in New Delhi. Contributing towards health and hygiene of our countrymen, we started the Cleanliness Drive with enthusiasm and swiftness.



On the auspicious occasion of Ganesh Chaturthi, we came across a canal in Rohini Sec18, Delhi which was long overdue and it was getting noxious for the ones living nearby. Our group of volunteers initiated the drive and dedicated themselves to this movement whole heartedly. Holding up the broom in hand and with determination in the heart to clean the dirt, to make Swachh Bharat Abhiyan a great success across the nation. We also tried to engage the locals to join in and be a part of this cleanliness drive and requested each of them to draw other people around them into the initiative.




Health camps are also held once or twice in a month where we provide free health check up and also create awareness about hygiene and cleanliness amongst the people
