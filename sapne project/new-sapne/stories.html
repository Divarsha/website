SUCCESS STORIES

Her name is Sapna, a young girl who recently migrated to Delhi from Nepal, along with her parents.The first few month in Delhi were tough.Having little knowledge of Hindi, the common language, Sapna struggled to communicate with other kids of her dilapidated locality in Delhi. But not only does she share her name with our organisation, she share SAPNE'S grit and determination as well!! Since the past three months,Sapnas been working diligently on her Hindi under the guidance of tutors provided by SAPNE. Her hardwork seems to be playing off and she can now converse and play with her friends with relative ease. During one of the career counselling sesions organised by SAPNE at her locality, Sapna shared a her dream of becoming a Doctor when she grows up. I want to help the needy she says with her trademark adorable smile.

Help her to fulfill her dreams.
